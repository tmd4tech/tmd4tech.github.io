### The problem
Rural electrification lives on a trade-off: the cheapest system is rarely the most reliable, and the most reliable is rarely affordable. Picking a point on that curve for a real community requires modelling, not intuition.

### What I built
Optimization models for hybrid solar-PV + storage + conventional systems that weigh reliability, cost, and operational trade-offs explicitly — sizing and dispatching systems for the constraints underserved communities actually face.

### Methodology
- Formulated system design and dispatch as an optimization problem over generation and storage.
- Encoded reliability targets and cost structures as competing objectives to be balanced, not assumed away.
- Produced configurations that make the reliability-vs-cost trade-off legible to decision-makers.
