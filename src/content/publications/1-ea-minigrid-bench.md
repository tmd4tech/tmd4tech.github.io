Energy planning in East Africa is bottlenecked less by algorithms than by data: high-resolution, labelled spatial data that machine-learning models can actually train on is scarce and fragmented. EA-MINIGRID-BENCH addresses this gap directly. We assemble a high-resolution spatial benchmark that pairs settlement, demand, and infrastructure signals across the region, and define standardized tasks and metrics so competing approaches to mini-grid siting and demand estimation can be compared on equal footing. The result is a reproducible foundation for machine-learning research on East African electrification — the common yardstick the field has been missing.
